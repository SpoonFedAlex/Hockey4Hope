THE MISSION

Hockey for Hope aims to provide underprivileged children the opportunity to learn about and play hockey. Hockey is a game that hones skills and teaches valuable life lessons such as teamwork, leadership, commitment, and dedication, but it is also an expensive game. With the cost of equipment, ice time, and travel expenses youth hockey can cost thousands of dollars per season. Hockey for Hope will look to close the expense gap that keeps many children from playing the game. The goal is for participating children to attend leadership classes and do community service in order to earn equipment and ice time. Everything provided to players will be earned, instilling the values of pride and ownership. Bringing the community together to subsidize a youth hockey organization will build a stronger future generation, which is essential for the turnaround of Detroit.
